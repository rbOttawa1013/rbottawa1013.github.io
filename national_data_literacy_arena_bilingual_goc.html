Dataset briefing challenge

Choose one of 10 challenge categories, review a real dataset link and complete a 10-question briefing quiz.

Privacy: use an alias only. No external AI or API is called.

How it works

  1. Choose a dataset category.
  2. Open and review the source dataset.
  3. Answer 10 briefing statements.
  4. Earn bronze, silver or gold.
  5. Review the leaderboard.
