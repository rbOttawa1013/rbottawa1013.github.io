My Canada, My Community

A student-friendly prototype for exploring a Canadian community through public geographic data, Statistics Canada source links, and guided research questions.

Alpha prototype · Not an official Government of Canada production service

Before you start

This page preloads a demonstration profile so the profile results, map, six viewpoints and research questions are available immediately. Postal-code search remains available to resolve another location and refresh the profile. The charts are for demonstration; use the linked Statistics Canada tables and profiles for authoritative figures.

Accessibility, privacy and data-quality gates

  • Privacy: the visible form asks for postal code only; it does not ask students for a full street address.
  • Accessibility: the interface uses visible labels, skip navigation, keyboard focus states, semantic landmarks and print support.
  • Data quality: generated charts are labelled as prototype outputs and source links are provided for verification.
  • Neutrality: prompts avoid partisan, religious and sensitive personal-attribute conclusions.
